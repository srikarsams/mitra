About

A privacy-first mutual fund portfolio tracker. Your statement is parsed entirely in this browser; no data is sent to any server except the public AMFI NAV endpoint.

NAV data via api.mfapi.in (community maintained, sourced from AMFI India).

Your data is stored in this browser. Clearing browser data will erase your portfolio. Re-import your CAS to restore.

How to get your CAS

Consolidated Account Statement

CAMS

  1. Visit camsonline.com
  2. Set From Date → 01-Jan-1990 (or your earliest investment date) and To Date → today. A partial range breaks XIRR and full returns.
  3. Choose Statement Type → Detailed (not Summary) and Password protected → Yes
  4. Enter your email and PAN; the PDF is sent by email

Password

Your PAN (uppercase) followed by date of birth as DDMMYYYY.
Example: ABCDE1234F01011990

Supported formats: CAMS Detailed CAS, CDSL eCAS, NSDL eCAS. KFintech CAS is not supported yet.
